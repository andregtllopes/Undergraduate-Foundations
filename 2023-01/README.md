# Semester 2023/1: Immersion and Mathematical Foundations

This folder marks the beginning of my journey in **Aerospace Engineering** at UFMG. This first semester was dedicated to establishing a rigorous analytical mindset and building the core mathematical foundations required for advanced engineering challenges.

## 📌 Overview
The primary focus of this period was the transition into higher education, emphasizing the development of logical-mathematical reasoning. It served as the "First-Principles" stage, where the fundamental laws of calculus, linear algebra, and physics were first formally explored.

## 🛠️ Key Academic Focus
* **Core Mathematics:** Intensive study of Calculus and Linear Algebra, providing the tools for future modeling in Deep Learning and Fluid Dynamics.
* **Aerospace Introduction:** First contact with the industry's history, aircraft classification, and basic systems engineering.
* **Technical Representation:** Initial exposure to technical standards and CAD (Computer-Aided Design) fundamentals.

## 🧠 Perspective
While most of the work in this semester was theoretical and focused on classroom performance, it provided the essential framework that supports my current research and simulation projects. There are no major software projects here, as the emphasis was on pen-and-paper mastery of engineering fundamentals.
